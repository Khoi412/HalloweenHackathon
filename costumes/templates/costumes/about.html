Who We Are

HauntHub is the UK’s ultimate Halloween and nightlife community — bringing together partygoers, music lovers, and thrill-seekers from all walks of (after)life. We host exclusive haunted raves and eerie experiences across the country, where beats meet the bizarre.

Whether you’re here to find your next spine-tingling event or to share your scariest costume moments, HauntHub is your home for all things dark, fun, and unforgettable.
